Emergency Services

Police, Ambulance and Firefighters are always at your disposable. Contact 911 whenever there is a life-threatening emergency.

Please Note: Our service updates wait times with a 10-minute delay. For urgent medical attention, call 911 or visit the nearest emergency department immediately. For health advice, call 811 to speak to a health services navigator.
